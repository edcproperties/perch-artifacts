# Call Prep — Tom Capabilities

Call Prep is the research and understanding Tom does **before and during** a sales call — enriching, gathering, summarizing, and preparing everything a rep needs to have a sharp conversation with a contractor. Each line is a capability: what Tom does.

## Capabilities

- **Rank the call list by a composite Perch Score** — blends Procore activity, typical job size, locality, ICP fit, and contactability into a single priority score.
- **Judge first-principles fit** — decide whether a company actually bids and scopes commercial construction (would ever get value from, or buy, Perch), and tag it Strong / Moderate / not-a-fit with a reason.
- **Enrich a company from the open web** — find and verify the real website, LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, founding year, employee count, and specialties, with a confidence rating.
- **Write a grounded TL;DR** — a source-verified, 2–3 sentence summary of what the company builds, its scale, and notable work. Never invented.
- **Size the company from real signals** — infer small / medium / large from employees, revenue, and scale cues, not misleading proxies like software-usage counts.
- **Surface the Procore signal** — total and currently-active projects, average job value, estimated volume, trades, and market sectors.
- **Assemble a single call card** — profile, click-to-call phone, enrichment, stats, and fit reason in one view.
- **Offer one-click research launchers** — the company's website, socials, Google, Maps, and Procore profile.
- **Auto-route the right call script** — by contractor type (GC vs. sub) and size (small / medium / large), with instant manual override.
- **Generate the discovery script** — opener variants (student / Jeremy / gatekeeper), the three core Mom-Test questions with follow-ups, size-tailored asks and "listen-for" cues, the matched GTM wedge, a one-question fallback, and an objection-handling library.
- **Flag data-quality catches found while enriching** — rebrands, misleading trade tags, wrong-entity matches, and out-of-area headquarters.
- **Search and filter the queue** — by name, trade, ICP, size, fit, and call status to work the right segment.
