Onboarding Decision Tree

the routing brain
→

The synthesis. The three walkthroughs collapse into one branching logic, from someone arriving down to the exact next question and the close. Eight gates (G0 to G7: identity, enrich, type, track, role, trade, next question, close), the four routes (cold or warm, self-serve or white-glove) traced through them, and the same tree rendered as a spec, the programmable backbone for the technical mapping. Built so we only ever ask what enrichment couldn't infer.

Trade-Config Library

all 34 trades
→

The structured levers the onboarding generator multiplies. Per trade: the /{trade} "{Trade} OS" lander, what Sarah extracts, the dollar lever, and the money flag (the change-order or lead-time gotcha). The 34 trades collapse into 6 takeoff families, so it's a finite library rather than a 150-cell matrix, and it feeds the marketing landers, the data model (TradeConfig), and Sarah's recipes all at once.
