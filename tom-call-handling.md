# Call Handling — Tom Capabilities

Call Handling is how Tom tracks the call, captures notes, and classifies the outcome — **during and after** the conversation. It turns each dial into clean, consistent, queryable data. Each line is a capability: what Tom does.

## Capabilities

- **Classify the call outcome** — into a standard disposition: productive, non-productive, gatekeeper, left voicemail, wrong person, advised-to-email, no answer, callback set, not a fit, or do-not-call.
- **Capture structured discovery notes** — contact and title, persona, current tools, objection raised, pain discovered, the question that landed, the verbatim "golden quote," next step, the GTM wedge that resonated, an interest rating, and free-form notes.
- **Keep a per-company call history** — a timestamped, reverse-chronological record of every touch.
- **Show live pipeline counters** — companies dialed, total calls, productive calls, and callbacks set.
- **Persist automatically** — every outcome, note, and script override saved per rep, with no login required.
- **Export the call log to CSV** — all calls plus structured fields, for the CRM or analysis.
- **Enforce a shared vocabulary** — consistent personas, tools, wedges, and outcomes so notes stay clean and queryable across the team.
- **Turn every logged call into structured, queryable discovery data** — persona × trade × objection × pain × quote × wedge — that can sharpen scripts and messaging over time.
