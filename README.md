# Perch Artifacts

Shareable, self-contained HTML artifacts for Perch (a complete operating system for commercial construction).

## Competitive Landscape

`index.html` — an interactive map of where Perch competes and where it could be flanked.

- 11 product categories grounded in Perch's actual functional areas
- 3-5 of the strongest competitors per category, grouped by their logos
- A threat lens (High / Medium / Low) plus a one-line "how they flank us" note per category
- Filters by threat level and by core-battleground vs. edge/add-on
- Click any logo for that rival's positioning and its angle of attack on Perch
- Editable per-category notes (saved to your browser via localStorage)

Logos live in `perch-competitive-logos/` and are referenced with relative paths, so the page is fully self-contained. Competitor sets were validated against 2026 market research; logos are the property of their respective owners.

Open `index.html` directly, or view the hosted version via GitHub Pages.
